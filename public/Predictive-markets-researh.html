The Election Supercycle

The predictive markets industry historically moves in four-year cycles, tethered to US Presidential Elections. However, 2024 marked a deviation from the norm: distinct growth in non-political verticals (economics, climate) indicates the industry is maturing beyond just "political betting."

While Polymarket dominated global volume due to crypto-accessibility, Kalshi's regulatory victory against the CFTC in late 2024 opened the floodgates for institutional US capital.

Platform Showdown: Kalshi vs. Polymarket

The market is bifurcated. Polymarket runs on the Polygon blockchain, offering global access, lower fees, and massive volume, but operates in a regulatory grey area for US users.

Kalshi is fully regulated by the CFTC. It offers direct USD deposits and legal certainty, attracting more conservative and institutional traders, though initially suffering from lower liquidity.

  • Polymarket: High Volume, Crypto-Native, Global
  • Kalshi: Regulated, USD Banking, US-Centric
