The Modern Student Athlete

Numbers, Influence, and the NIL Economy

The landscape of college sports has shifted fundamentally. Beyond the touchdowns and buzzers, a new economy has emerged. We analyzed data across 350+ Division 1 universities to understand the average student-athlete's reality: from their population on campus to their digital footprint and how they are monetizing their Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL).

The "Star Power" Gap

It is crucial to visualize the disparity in the NIL market. The "Average" is pulled up significantly by the top 1% of athletes (Quarterbacks, Point Guards, Social Media Stars).

  • ● The Top 1%: Millions in followers, national brand deals.
  • ● The Middle Class: 10k-50k followers, local partnerships, free product.
  • ● The Field: <5k followers, micro-influencers, barter deals.
