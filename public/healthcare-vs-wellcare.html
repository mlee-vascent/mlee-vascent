Sick Care vs. Well Care

A comparative economic analysis of the Traditional US Healthcare System and the emerging Health & Wellness Economy.

The Trillion Dollar Gap

While the Wellness economy is booming, it is still dwarfed by the massive scale of traditional healthcare spending. The US Healthcare system, driven by insurance and hospital care, commands trillions annually, whereas the Wellness market (consumer-driven) operates in the billions.

The Price of Expertise

A look at the financial incentives for professionals. Specialized medicine commands the highest salaries due to education requirements and insurance reimbursement models. Wellness earnings are more variable, often dependent on business ownership or celebrity clientele.
